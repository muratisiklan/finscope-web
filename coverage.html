Brokerage connections · SnapTrade

Connect your accounts, read-only

Portfoyo links your brokerages through SnapTrade, a regulated account aggregator. Connections are strictly read-only — Portfoyo imports your accounts, positions, and buy/sell history, and can never place a trade or move money.

What syncs: accounts & balances, current positions, and your buy/sell transaction history. What never happens: trading, transfers, or any change to your brokerage. Disconnect and wipe a link anytime.

This is a representative list of SnapTrade's supported institutions. Brokerage support and availability evolve — the live, connectable list always shows in the app at launch.
