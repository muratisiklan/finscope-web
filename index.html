How it works

From scattered accounts to one clear picture

Three steps to the truth behind your net worth — connected or manual, it all lands in the same clean view.

01

Connect or add

Link supported brokerages through SnapTrade — strictly read-only — or add any holding by hand: stocks, crypto, cash, commodities and more.

02

See the truth

Licensed prices value every position with an honest freshness label, then Portfoyo computes your real net worth and true realized vs. unrealized performance.

03

Track & decide

Read your allocation, concentration, dividend income and monthly recaps — so you rebalance and act from a clear picture, not a guess.

01 Every holding, in depth

Tap any holding — see the whole story

Open any position for a real-time, native-currency quote with an honest freshness label, your cost basis and today's move, plus the full buy/sell history behind it.

  • Real-time price, clearly tagged real-time / end-of-day
  • Your position — value, today's change, weighted-avg cost
  • Full transaction history, realized vs. unrealized split
  • Key stats — market cap, P/E, 52-week range, dividend yield
02 Brokerage sync · SnapTrade

Link your accounts, read-only

Portfoyo connects through SnapTrade, a regulated aggregator, to import your accounts, positions, and buy/sell activity. The connection is strictly read-only — Portfoyo can never place a trade or move your money. Refresh on demand, and reconnect in a tap if a link ever drops.

  • 35+ brokerages across the US, Canada, Europe, Australia & India
  • Account types: Individual, Roth/Traditional IRA, Joint, Margin, 401(k), Crypto
  • On-demand refresh & one-tap reconnect

03 Dividend income

The cash your portfolio pays you

Most trackers stop at price. Portfoyo projects the income your holdings generate — what's coming, who pays it, and how healthy that stream is — so you can build toward cash flow, not just gains.

  • Projected annual income, yield & yield-on-cost
  • 12-month payout history & an upcoming payments countdown
  • Top payers ranked, with an income-health read on concentration
04 Allocation & concentration

See exactly how you're balanced

An instant visual breakdown by asset class, sector, and account, across everything you hold. Switch the donut between views and read your concentration at a glance — so you rebalance from a clear picture instead of a guess.

  • Donut by position, asset type, or sector
  • Concentration stats — Top & Top 3 weight
  • Breakdown by account, all in one place
05 Performance

Realized vs. unrealized, never mixed

Active holdings stay separate from positions you've already sold — so you see how your open portfolio is doing and how your past decisions actually played out. Cost basis is computed as a weighted average from your real ledger, not a guess.

  • Open vs. closed positions, each with its own P/L
  • Weighted-average cost basis & ROI
  • Performance history charted over time

Built private

Your portfolio is yours alone

A finance app should earn trust before it asks for data. Here's how Portfoyo is built.

Read-only connections

Brokerage links can view holdings and activity only — they can never place a trade or move your money.

Sign in with Apple

Sign in with Apple or email. Sessions are encrypted on-device, and each row of your data is isolated to your account.

Delete everything, anytime

One tap in the app permanently wipes your account and all of its data from our servers. No emails, no waiting.

We don't sell your data

Your financial data is never sold or shared for advertising. Portfoyo makes money from premium, not from you.

Not a broker or adviser

Portfoyo is a tracking tool. It doesn't hold your assets, execute trades, or give investment advice.

Licensed, encrypted data

Market data is licensed for in-app display, and all traffic is encrypted in transit between your device and our servers.

Good to know

Questions, answered

When does it launch?

We're in private beta now and launching on iOS soon. Join the waitlist and we'll email you the moment it's live — early members get first access.

Which brokerages can I connect?

Portfoyo connects through SnapTrade, which supports 35+ brokerages across the US, Canada, Europe, Australia, and India — including Robinhood, Schwab, Fidelity, Vanguard, E*TRADE, Wealthsimple, Questrade, Webull, Trading 212, Interactive Brokers, and crypto exchanges like Coinbase and Kraken. See the full list →

Is my brokerage data safe?

Connections are read-only — Portfoyo can see your holdings and activity but can never trade or move money. Linking runs through SnapTrade, a regulated aggregator, and your data is tied only to your account.

Are the prices live?

Prices come from Twelve Data on a licensed business plan. US stocks/ETFs are real-time during market hours; crypto, forex and commodities are real-time around the clock; and Europe, the UK, Canada, India and 70+ other exchanges are end-of-day at launch — every holding is labeled with its exact status. See coverage →

Do I have to connect a brokerage?

No. You can track everything manually — stocks, crypto, cash, commodities, bonds, FX — and only connect brokerages if you want automatic syncing.

Does it track dividends?

Yes. Portfoyo projects your annual income, yield and yield-on-cost, charts twelve months of payouts, counts down upcoming payments, ranks your top payers, and reads income health for concentration and sustainability.

Will it cost anything?

The core tracker is free. A premium tier will come later — we're still shaping exactly what's in it. As a thank-you, every waitlist member gets a code for 50% off the yearly plan when premium launches.

Do you sell my data?

No. Your portfolio is yours. We don't sell or share your personal financial data, and you can delete your account and all of its data from inside the app at any time.
