Frequently Asked Questions

Does FinScope connect to my bank?
No. FinScope is a manual tracker. You must enter every transaction (buy/sell) yourself. This ensures complete privacy and control over your data.
What happens if I forget my password?
Please record your password safely. Since we do not verify email addresses or store personal identity data, we cannot reset your password. If you lose it, you will lose access to your account.
Is the market data real-time?
No. You enter the "Current Price" manually to update your portfolio value. This is designed for long-term investors who want to check their net worth periodically, not day traders.
