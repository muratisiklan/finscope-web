Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2025

FinScope ("we," "our," or "us") provides a manual portfolio tracking service. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Identity Data: We collect your Email Address and a chosen Username solely to create and secure your account.
  • User-Generated Financial Data: We store the investment transactions (tickers, quantities, prices, dates) that you manually enter into the app. This data is stored on our secure servers to provide you with analytics across your devices.
  • No Automatic Data Collection: We do not connect to bank accounts, brokerages, or third-party market data providers. All financial data in the app is provided by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide analytical calculations and portfolio visualizations based on your input.
  • To manage your account and subscription status via RevenueCat.
  • To maintain app performance and stability.

We do not use your email for marketing campaigns, newsletters, or third-party advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate the app:

  • RevenueCat: Handles subscription validation and purchase history processing. Read Policy.
  • Expo: Provides the underlying infrastructure for the mobile application. Read Policy.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only as long as you have an active account. You may delete your account and all associated data permanently at any time via the Profile > Delete Account button within the app.

5. Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your account credentials and stored data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact: support@finscope.app
