Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026 · Effective: June 26, 2026

The short version. Portfoyo is a portfolio tracker. We collect the minimum we need to run your account: your email, the holdings and transactions you add or import, and what you choose to connect. Brokerage links are made through SnapTrade and are strictly read-only — we can never trade or move your money, and we never see or store your brokerage username or password. We do not sell your data, and we do not use advertising or third-party tracking. You can export or permanently delete your account and all of its data from inside the app at any time. This page explains the details.

1. Who we are

Portfoyo (“Portfoyo”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is a personal investment portfolio tracking application for mobile devices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Portfoyo mobile app and the website at portfoyo.app (together, the “Service”).

For the purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”), the UK GDPR, and the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 (“KVKK”), Portfoyo is the data controller of the personal data described here. You can reach us about any privacy matter at support@portfoyo.app.

2. Scope of this policy

This policy covers the Portfoyo app and website. It does not cover the practices of third parties we integrate with — such as your brokerage, SnapTrade, Twelve Data, Apple, or our infrastructure providers — except to describe what data flows between us. Those parties operate under their own privacy policies, which we link to in Section 8.

3. Data we collect

We collect only the data needed to provide the Service. This falls into the following categories.

a) Account & identity data

  • Email address — used to create your account, sign you in, verify your identity, and send essential service messages (e.g. email confirmation and password reset codes).
  • Password — authentication is handled by our backend provider (Supabase Auth). Your password is stored only as a salted cryptographic hash; we never store or have access to your plaintext password.
  • Display name (optional) — a name you may set for your profile. If you don’t set one, we default to the part of your email before the “@”.

b) Profile & preferences

  • Your chosen display currency and in-app settings/preferences.

c) Financial data you add or import

  • Manual holdings & transactions you enter — asset names/tickers, quantities, cost basis, buy/sell activity, prices, fees, and dates.
  • Imported brokerage data (only if you connect an account — see Section 4) — your account names and types, an account mask (typically the last 4 digits), account currency, positions/holdings, transaction history, and account values.
  • Derived analytics we calculate from the above — for example net worth, asset allocation, realized vs. unrealized performance, dividend projections, and daily portfolio value snapshots.

d) Subscription data

  • If you purchase a premium subscription, your purchase is processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat. We receive your subscription entitlement status (e.g. tier, active/expired, renewal date) linked to your account. We never receive your payment card or billing details — those stay with Apple.

e) Technical & operational data

  • Authentication tokens — a session token issued at sign-in, stored securely on your device in the system keychain/keystore.
  • App & update data — the app uses Expo’s over-the-air update service, which processes limited technical information (such as app version and runtime/platform) to deliver the correct update.
  • Operational logs — minimal server-side records of sync jobs and errors, used to keep syncing reliable and to diagnose problems.

Portfoyo does not include advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or cross-app tracking, and does not ask for App Tracking Transparency permission. We do not build advertising profiles about you.

4. Brokerage connections (SnapTrade)

Connecting a brokerage is entirely optional — you can use Portfoyo with only manually entered data. If you do choose to connect, the link is made through SnapTrade, a regulated financial-data aggregator.

  • Read-only by design. Connections grant read-only access to your holdings and activity. Portfoyo cannot place trades, transfer funds, or make any changes to your brokerage account.
  • We never see your brokerage credentials. You enter your brokerage login directly with SnapTrade (or your brokerage’s own secure login). Your username and password are never transmitted to, seen by, or stored by Portfoyo.
  • What we store is a SnapTrade connection identifier and an access secret/token that lets us refresh your holdings on your behalf. This secret is treated as sensitive and is encrypted at rest.
  • You stay in control. You can disconnect a brokerage at any time from inside the app, which revokes the authorization and stops further syncing.

SnapTrade processes your data under its own privacy policy: snaptrade.com/privacy.

5. Market data (Twelve Data)

Prices, fundamentals, dividend schedules, and exchange-rate data are provided by Twelve Data under a licensed business plan. This is general market data and is not specific to you: when we fetch a quote we send only the security symbol — we do not send your identity, account, or portfolio to the market-data provider. See Twelve Data’s policy at twelvedata.com/privacy.

6. How we use your data

  • To create and authenticate your account and keep you signed in securely.
  • To store, sync, and display your holdings and transactions across your devices.
  • To calculate and present portfolio analytics (net worth, allocation, performance, dividends).
  • To import and keep your connected brokerage data up to date (if you connect one).
  • To manage premium subscriptions and unlock entitlements.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse and fraud, and debug and improve reliability.
  • To respond to your support requests and communicate essential service notices.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

We do not use your personal financial data to train advertising models or to profile you for marketing.

7. Legal bases for processing (GDPR & KVKK)

Where the GDPR, UK GDPR, or KVKK applies, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract — to provide the account, syncing, and analytics you sign up for.
  • Consent — for optional actions such as connecting a brokerage account or subscribing to the waitlist. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests — to secure the Service, prevent fraud and abuse, and improve reliability, balanced against your rights.
  • Legal obligation — where we must retain or disclose data to comply with applicable law.

9. We do not sell or “share” your data

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not “share” it for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are defined under California law. We do not exchange your financial data for money or other consideration.

10. International data transfers

Portfoyo and our providers may process and store data on servers located outside your country, including in the European Union and the United States. Where personal data is transferred internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses or an adequacy decision. By using the Service, you understand that your information may be processed in these locations.

11. Data retention

  • We retain your account and portfolio data for as long as your account is active.
  • When you delete your account (Profile → Delete Account in the app), we permanently delete your profile, holdings, transactions, connections, and associated personal data from our live systems.
  • Residual copies in encrypted backups are purged on our regular backup-rotation cycle (typically within 30 days).
  • We may retain a minimal record where required for legal, accounting, fraud-prevention, or dispute-resolution purposes, kept only for as long as necessary.

When your account is closed or the Service ends

In addition to voluntary deletion, your data may be affected in the following situations:

  • You close your account. Deletion is initiated by you in the app and is permanent for your portfolio data, as described above. Active brokerage links are revoked and SnapTrade authorizations are disconnected as part of this process.
  • We suspend, terminate, or shut down the Service. We may restrict or end your access, or discontinue Portfoyo (in whole or in part), at our sole discretion and with or without prior notice. We are not required to give reasons or a grace period except where mandatory law requires. Unless we must retain information for legal or dispute-resolution reasons, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period (typically within 30 days) after access ends.
  • We discontinue the product. If Portfoyo is shut down, we may do so without advance notice unless applicable law requires otherwise. Where practicable and not prohibited, we may notify users by email or in-app message, but we are not obligated to do so.
  • Waitlist emails. If you only joined the waitlist and never created an app account, email us at support@portfoyo.app to request removal at any time.
  • Subscriptions. Deleting your Portfoyo account does not automatically cancel an Apple App Store subscription. You must cancel recurring billing separately in your Apple ID subscription settings.

12. How we protect your data

  • Encryption in transit — all communication between the app and our servers uses TLS/HTTPS.
  • Encryption at rest — data is stored on encrypted infrastructure, and sensitive secrets (such as your SnapTrade connection token) are additionally encrypted.
  • Secure session storage — your sign-in session is stored in the device’s hardware-backed keychain/keystore, never in plaintext.
  • Row-level isolation — database access rules ensure each user can only read and write their own data.
  • Least privilege — brokerage access is read-only, and we request only the data we need.

No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your data using industry-standard safeguards and to continuously improve them.

13. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have some or all of the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data (much of which you can edit directly in the app).
  • Erasure — delete your account and data, which you can do yourself at any time in the app.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Restriction & objection — restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw consent where processing is based on it (e.g. disconnect a brokerage, unsubscribe from the waitlist).

To exercise any right, email support@portfoyo.app. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law (generally within 30 days). You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority — in the EU/EEA with your national supervisory authority, in the UK with the ICO, or in Türkiye with the KVKK Authority (KVKK Kurumu).

14. Additional rights for U.S. & California residents

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (as amended by the CPRA) gives you the right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it, to request access to and deletion of that information, to correct inaccurate information, and to not be discriminated against for exercising your rights. Because we do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, there is no need to opt out of such activity. You may exercise these rights by emailing us at the address above; we will verify your request using your account email.

15. Children

Portfoyo is intended for adults and is not directed to children. The Service is not available to anyone under 18 years of age (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

16. The website & the waitlist

  • The marketing website does not use advertising or third-party analytics cookies.
  • If you join the waitlist, we collect the email address you submit, plus minimal anti-spam metadata, so we can notify you about launch and early access. We use a hidden honeypot field to deter automated submissions.
  • You can unsubscribe or ask us to delete your waitlist email at any time by contacting us.

17. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the Service or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app or by email. Your continued use of the Service after an update means you accept the revised policy.

18. Contact us

For any questions, requests, or concerns about this policy or your data, contact us at support@portfoyo.app. We read every message and aim to respond promptly.
