Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2025

Portfoyo ("we," "our," or "us") operates a manual portfolio tracking application. This policy describes how we handle your data with a strict focus on privacy and data minimization.

1. Information We Collect

We adhere to the principle of Data Minimization. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or password.

  • Anonymous Identity: When you install the app, we generate a random unique identifier (UUID) internally. This ID is used to sync your data but is not visible to you.
  • Financial Data: We store the investment transaction data (asset names, prices, quantities, dates) that you manually enter on our secure cloud servers. This data is associated only with your internal Anonymous ID.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous crash reports or performance metrics to improve app stability.

2. No Market Data or Banking APIs

We do not connect to any third-party banking APIs or market data providers. We do not have access to your actual bank accounts or brokerage statements. All financial data in the app is user-generated.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate and display portfolio analytics (e.g., Net Worth, Asset Allocation).
  • To sync your manual entries securely across your devices.
  • To validate your Premium subscription status via RevenueCat using your Anonymous ID.

4. Third-Party Services

We share minimal data with the following trusted partners solely for app functionality:

  • RevenueCat: Processes subscription receipts. We send your anonymous User ID to link your purchase to your app instance. Read Policy.
  • Expo: Provides the underlying app infrastructure. Read Policy.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as the app is installed on your device. You have the right to delete your data at any time:

  • Deletion via App Removal: If you uninstall the application, the internal key required to access your data is destroyed. Consequently, your data becomes permanently inaccessible.
  • Explicit Deletion: Go to Profile > Delete Account within the app. This action permanently wipes your Anonymous ID and all associated transaction data from our servers immediately. This cannot be undone.

6. Security

We use SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission. Since we do not collect personal identifiers like emails or passwords, there are no traditional credentials to be stolen. However, you are responsible for keeping your physical device secure to prevent unauthorized access to your app data.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information, including anonymized financial data, may be maintained on computers located outside of your jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

8. Your Rights (KVKK & GDPR)

Under applicable data protection laws (including KVKK and GDPR), you have rights regarding your data. However, because we do not collect personal identifiers and the Anonymous ID is not visible to you, we cannot verify your identity outside of the app itself.

  • To exercise your Right to Erasure or Right to Access, you must use the tools provided directly within the application (such as the "Delete Account" button). We cannot process these requests via email as we have no way to locate your specific data without a personal identifier.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 18 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 18.

10. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries, please contact: portfoyo.app@outlook.com
